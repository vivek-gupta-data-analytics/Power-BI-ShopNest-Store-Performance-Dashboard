# ShopNest Store Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is a Power BI dashboard built for the ShopNest Store Capstone Project. The dashboard analyzes business performance across product sales, delayed deliveries, payment methods, customer ratings, regional sales distribution, quarterly sales patterns, and yearly revenue growth.

The objective of this project was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard that supports clear performance tracking and data-driven decision-making.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features

- KPI cards for high-level business summary
- Interactive slicers for filtering dashboard views
- Bar and column charts for category, delivery, and regional analysis
- Donut chart for payment method analysis
- Line charts for quarterly sales and yearly revenue trends
- Drillthrough page for monthly delayed vs on-time delivery analysis

## Key Analysis Areas

- Top product categories by total sales
- Delayed orders by product category
- Monthly comparison of delayed and on-time orders
- Customer payment method preference
- Highest-rated and lowest-rated category analysis
- State-wise sales performance
- Quarterly seasonal sales patterns
- Yearly revenue growth trend

## Key Insights

- Health & Beauty was the highest-performing category in the selected 2017 dashboard view.
- Delayed orders were concentrated in high-volume categories such as Health & Beauty and Bed Bath Table.
- Credit Card was the most frequently used payment method.
- Sales were geographically concentrated, with SP contributing the highest sales.
- Sales performance showed quarter-wise seasonal variation.
- Revenue showed a strong upward trend from 2016 to 2018.

## Business Impact

The dashboard helps identify revenue-driving categories, delivery delay patterns, customer payment behavior, regional sales concentration, seasonal demand movement, and long-term revenue growth. These insights can support better decision-making in sales strategy, logistics monitoring, customer experience improvement, and business performance tracking.
